What Does This Mean For Your Business?

How to Reduce Absenteeism Costs:

    • Implement comprehensive wellness programs

    • Enhance workplace ergonomics and comfort

    • Offer flexible work arrangements (e.g., hybrid schedules)

    • Foster a positive and inclusive workplace culture

    • Set clear attendance expectations and policies

    • Provide training for managers to proactively address absenteeism
